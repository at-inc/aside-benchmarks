average?\nWhen trying to [lose weight](https://www.healthline.com/nutrition/weight-management) , it’s important [to create](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC8017325/) a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than usual or exercising more. Some people choose to combine the two, eating a little less while being more physically active.\nHowever, it’s important to ensure that you’re consuming enough nutrients, even if you’re trying to lose weight.
