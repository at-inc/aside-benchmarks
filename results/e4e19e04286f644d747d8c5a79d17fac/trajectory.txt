**Report:** Drug Interaction Report: Alcohol (contained in alcoholic beverages), Viagra  
**URL:** https://www.drugs.com/interactions-check.php?drug_list=1034-14582,2061-1352

**Main interaction:** **Moderate**  
Drugs.com says sildenafil (Viagra) can lower blood pressure, and alcohol may increase that effect. Possible symptoms include dizziness, lightheadedness, fainting, flushing, headache, and heart palpitations.

**Their guidance:** Avoid or limit alcohol to **no more than 4 alcohol-containing drinks during a small period of time** while taking sildenafil, and use caution when standing up. Always check with a healthcare provider before making medication changes.
