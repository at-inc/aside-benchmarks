[10] assistant: I opened the Drugs.com Humira Dosage Guide: https://www.drugs.com/dosage/humira.html

Key dosage info shown there:

- **RA, Psoriatic Arthritis, Ankylosing Spondylitis:** 40 mg subcutaneously every other week. Some RA patients not taking methotrexate may use 40 mg weekly or 80 mg every other week.
- **Adult Crohn’s / Ulcerative Colitis:** 160 mg Day 1, 80 mg Day 15, then 40 mg every other week starting Day 29.
- **Plaque Psoriasis / Adult Uveitis:** 80 mg once, then 40 mg every other week starting 1 week later.
- **Hidradenitis Suppurativa adults:** 160 mg Day 1, 80 mg Day 15, then 40 mg weekly or 80 mg every other week from Day 29.
- **Pediatric dosing:** varies by condition and weight.

Drugs.com also notes Humira should be used under physician guidance, with TB evaluation before and during therapy.
