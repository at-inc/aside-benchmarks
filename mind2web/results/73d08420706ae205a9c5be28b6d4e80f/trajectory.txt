### Reservation rules
- **Maximum consecutive stay:** up to **14 nights** per visit.
- **Maximum stay:** up to **14 total nights** within a rolling 30-day period, as displayed on the site.
- **Max reservations per transaction:** up to **4 reservations** in one credit-card transaction.
- **After arrival modifications:** allowed online through **My Reservations** for remaining nights, including changing sites or leaving early. Past nights cannot be changed.

### Changes
- A **$10 change fee per reserved site** applies if the new stay dates are completely different.
- No extra fee for:
  - Adding or removing nights
  - Changing to a different campsite
- Balance adjusts based on the nightly rate.

### Cancellations
- You can cancel any time before scheduled arrival.
- Refund is reduced by:
  - **$10 cancellation fee**
  - Any reservation fees paid when booking
  - Any previous change fees
- **Late cancellation:**
  - Individual campsites: starts at midnight **1 calendar day before check-in**
  - Cabins/group sites: starts at midnight **14 calendar days or fewer before check-in**
  - Late cancellations also lose the **first night’s recreation use fee**

### Day-use rule
- The regular modification/cancellation/refund rules do **not** apply to day-use sites.
- Day-use reservations forfeit the day-use recreation fee if cancelled **less than 14 days before arrival**.
